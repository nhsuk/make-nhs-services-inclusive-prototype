Make NHS services inclusive

These drafts are a collaborative effort by teams across NHS England.

Inclusive design definition

Inclusive design is an approach to designing and delivering NHS services so that everyone can access them regardless of their background or life experience.

Find out more about what we mean by inclusive design.

Why inclusive design is important

We build inclusive services that everyone can use.

Read more about what we mean by inclusion and why it's important.

Things teams need to do

  • Consider the experiences of groups who are often excluded

  • Conduct inclusive research

  • Use words and images that are inclusive

  • Change the way you design and build things

  • Manage relationships
