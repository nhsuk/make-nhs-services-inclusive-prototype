Make NHS services inclusive

These drafts are a collaborative effort by teams across NHS Digital. We're starting with a private alpha so we can iterate things quickly and often.

Why it's important

We build inclusive services that everyone can use. Read more about what we mean by inclusion and why it's important.

Things teams need to do

  • Consider the experiences of groups who are often excluded

  • Conduct inclusive research

  • Use words and images that are inclusive

  • Change the way you design and build things

  • Manage relationships
