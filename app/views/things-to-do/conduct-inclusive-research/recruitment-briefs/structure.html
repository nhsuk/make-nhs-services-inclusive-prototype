Structure

Some intro blurb here about how this is a template that can be used for a range of situations.

Hi,

We are looking to conduct [insert number, and length] research sessions via [Teams, Zoom, etc.,] on [insert date or week].

We are working on [this service, this team etc] and we need to hear from people who [key user group determiner of the service, e.g., have booked GP appointments digitally].

An example of what we are looking for:

  • Age range
  • Location / residency [do they have to live in England?]
  • Gender mix? [explain the mix, and how important this is]
  • Work status or job role [not working, GP administrator]
  • Any habits or specifications that are relevant to the research [e.g., they are NHS App users, they are carers for their parents]
  • Any experience with NHS services? [does anything eliminate them?]
  • Technological literacy? [comfortable using the internet, not comfortable at allâ€¦
  • Access needs?

Exclusions

List all things that means that they cannot participate in the research, for example:

  • No health workers
  • No previous experience of NHS research

Additional / nice to have requests

  • It would be great if you could find some who [x], but this is not essential.

Please let me know if this is something you can help with, and how much you think it should cost.

Thank you,

Examples

Mention these are just to illustrate how to use the structure above?.

Example

3 x participants who have never been invited to breast cancer screening or attended a mammogram:

  • Aged 40 - 52
  • Mix of socio-economic groups
  • Mix of work status (full time, part time, not working)
  • At least 1x participants whose first language is not English
  • At least 1x participant who use a wheelchair or has difficulty standing for long periods of time and uses walking aids (eg a stick)
  • At least 1x participant whose mother or sister has had breast cancer

3 x participants who have attended breast cancer screening at least once:

  • Aged 50 - 65
  • Mix of socio-economic groups
  • Mix of work status (full time, part time, not working)
  • At least 1x participants whose first language is not English
  • At least 1x participant who use a wheelchair or has difficulty standing for long periods of time and uses walking aids (eg a stick)

Exclusions

  • None of the participants should be cis-male
  • None of the participants should have had breast cancer in the past or be undergoing treatment for breast cancer
  • None of the participants should work as healthcare professionals, but people working in social care or as careworkers can be included
  • None of the participants should work in IT, marketing, media, design or communications roles
  • No more than 3 participants should be white british

Additional request

  • If possible it would be great to have one participant who has breast implants.
Example

Participant criteria:

  • 2 participants with access needs required.
  • Ideally from a range of socio-economic backgrounds.
  • Ideally have a mix of technical confidence.
  • Must be aged 18+ (mix of age ranges if possible).
  • Must have access to their own iOS (Apple) or Android smartphone.
  • Must have used an NHS service (e.g. GP, hospital, pharmacy, dentist, optician) within the last 12 months.
  • Must not have worked for NHS or in healthcare in the past 5 years.
  • Must not have worked in digital, IT or technology professions in the past 5 years.
  • Must not have participated in any NHS research within the last 12 months.
