Research with trans people

Contributors

When you introduce yourself, tell the person your pronouns

This normalises asking for pronouns, and allows everyone to say how they would like to be addressed.

Never assume someone's pronouns.

If you are showing non-inclusive content, give people trigger warnings

This can be as simple as a verbal warning like "We're going to show you a page on breastfeeding now, which might be triggering. If at any point you need to leave or stop, please do so".

This is important because:

  • people may need to mentally prepare themselves to deal with it
  • people might not feel able to deal with the content during the session and may have to stop, in which case it's important that you support them to do this
